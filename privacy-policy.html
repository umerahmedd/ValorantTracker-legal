Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

1. About this app

Valorant Tracker is a third-party companion app for VALORANT players. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Riot Games, Inc. Riot Games and VALORANT are trademarks or registered trademarks of Riot Games, Inc.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use Valorant Tracker.

2. Riot data that we access

When you choose to link your Riot Account through Riot Sign On (RSO), you authorize us to access certain game and account data from Riot Games on your behalf via the official Riot Games API. This data is necessary to provide the stats, insights, and tracking features of the app.

The Riot-related data we may collect includes:

  • Your Riot Account identifiers, such as your Riot ID, game name, tagline, PUUID (player universally unique identifier), and linked region.
  • Your VALORANT profile and ranked data, including current rank, rank rating (RR / MMR), competitive tier, peak rank, and act history.
  • Your match history and match statistics, such as match results, agent selections, kills, deaths, assists, scores, economy, and other in-game performance metrics made available by Riot.
  • Your party and social identifiers only where exposed by the Riot API to enable features you explicitly use.

We do not collect or store your Riot password. Authentication is handled securely through Riot's OAuth flow, and any access tokens are stored only on your device for as long as needed to refresh your stats.

We only access the data that Riot makes available to us and that you authorize through RSO. You can review the data Riot provides at any time in your Riot Account settings and on the official Riot Games Privacy Notice.

3. Other information we collect

  • Device and usage information: device type, operating system version, app version, anonymized crash logs, and in-app usage patterns used to improve stability and user experience.
  • Support information: if you contact us for help, we collect your email address and any details you choose to provide.
  • Purchase information: if the app offers premium features, transaction details are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store and are subject to their respective privacy policies.

4. How we use your information

  • To display your VALORANT stats, match history, rank progression, and other analytics inside the app.
  • To personalize your dashboard and saved preferences (e.g., favorite agents, tracked metrics).
  • To improve app performance, diagnose crashes, and develop new features.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the security and integrity of the app.

5. Legal basis for processing

We process Riot account and game data on the basis of performing our contract with you (providing the app's features) and, where applicable, our legitimate interest in maintaining a secure and reliable service. We process support data based on your consent or to perform our contract with you.

6. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Riot Games: We exchange data with Riot Games solely to authenticate your account and retrieve the game data you authorize. Riot's handling of your data is governed by the Riot Games Privacy Notice and Terms of Service.
  • Service providers: We may use trusted providers for hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and customer support. They are contractually bound to process data only on our behalf and for the purposes described here.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain your Riot-related data for as long as your account remains linked to Valorant Tracker and as needed to provide the service. If you unlink your Riot Account or delete the app, we will delete or anonymize your Riot data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to keep it for legal, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.

8. Your choices and control

  • Unlink your Riot Account: You can revoke access at any time inside the app's Settings or through your Riot Account settings on the web.
  • Delete your data: You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Opt out of communications: You can stop receiving non-essential communications by following the unsubscribe instructions or contacting us.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure transmission via HTTPS, encrypted storage of access tokens where available, and limited access to backend systems. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's privacy

Valorant Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required to use Riot Services in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify you through the app or by email where appropriate.
